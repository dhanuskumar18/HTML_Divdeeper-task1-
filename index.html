Calisthenics

  • Calisthenics is a form of exercise that involves using your own body weight for resistance to build strength, flexibility, and endurance. It's a highly versatile and effective workout method that can be performed with minimal equipment. Here are some key points about calisthenics:
  • Key Exercises:
    1. Push-Ups: A foundational exercise for building upper body strength, particularly in the chest, shoulders, and triceps.
    2. Pull-Ups/Chin-Ups: Great for developing upper body strength, particularly in the back and biceps.
    3. Dips: Focus on the triceps, chest, and shoulders.
    4. Squats: Essential for building leg strength and endurance.
    5. Lunges: Target the legs and glutes while also improving balance and coordination.
